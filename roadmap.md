# Roadmap (Planned, Not Yet Implemented)

This roadmap represents the intended expansion of the platform. Items in this
document are not current features.

## Near Term
- Automated enrichment at lead ingestion.
- More intelligence sources and evidence weighting.
- Enhanced email extraction and fallback strategies.
- Policy-based confidence thresholds for outreach.

## Mid Term
- Autonomous sales execution (agent-led sequencing with escalation).
- Multi-channel outreach (SMS, LinkedIn).
- Appointment booking and calendar sync.
- A/B testing of outreach strategies.
- Performance analytics for conversion rates.

## Long Term
- Client onboarding workflows and policy templates.
- Finance and operations automation (invoicing, payment tracking, reporting).
- Multi-tenant platform with role-based access control.
- Agent marketplace and custom agent strategies.
