# Hiring Manager Summary

LifesaverTech AI Agency is a production-grade AI orchestration system designed
to run the core operations of Lifesaver Technology Services, a local AI
automation consulting agency. Built with modern AI engineering practices, the
system separates reasoning (LLMs) from execution (tools) and centers around a
deterministic orchestrator that enforces policy compliance, maintains workflow
memory, and logs every decision for auditability.

The end goal is an agent-operated consulting business where lead intelligence,
outreach, onboarding, and operations workflows are progressively automated
under deterministic control and auditability. Squads act as bounded business
units, enabling multiple AI-operated teams to run in parallel with isolated
policies and memory.

## Why This Stands Out
- **Control-plane architecture:** LLMs propose, the orchestrator decides.
- **Policy-gated autonomy:** deterministic rules for DNC, frequency, channel,
  time-of-day, and readiness thresholds.
- **Auditable workflows:** replayable decisions with append-only event logs.
- **Engineering rigor:** clear module boundaries, idempotent executors, and
  queue-based dispatch to avoid blocking API responses.

## What It Does Today
- Lead discovery via Google Places.
- Ethical web enrichment and evidence-based intelligence extraction.
- AI readiness scoring with confidence and reasoning.
- Outreach execution via Vapi (voice) and n8n (follow-up workflows).
- Dashboard visibility into lead intelligence and workflow state.

## Why It Matters
This system solves a real go-to-market bottleneck: sales teams need deep context
and safe automation without compliance risk. The architecture demonstrates
senior-level judgment in balancing autonomy with determinism, safety, and
observability.
